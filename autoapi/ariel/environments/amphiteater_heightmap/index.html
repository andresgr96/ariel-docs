Spawn a robot inside the amphitheater world.

Parameters:

  • mj_spec (mujoco.MjSpec) – The MuJoCo specification for the robot.

  • spawn_position (list[float] | None) – Optional spawn position in the world. If None, defaults to [0, 0, 0].

  • small_gap (float) – Small gap to avoid collision with the terrain.

  • correct_for_bounding_box (bool) – Whether to adjust the spawn position based on the bounding box.
